What is Watervale?

Since 1917, Watervale has brought families and friends back together, year after year. This amazing place located on Lake Michigan in Northern Michigan holds a special place in many people's hearts. Consisting of 20 weekly rental cottages, Watervale creates unforgettable memories each summer. Each cottage has a unique history and personality. Celebrating 100 years, we ask you to join us and experience the Watervale community!

Watervale History

Watervale started as a lumber town in 1892. The town ran as a lumber mill for a few years until the town was abandoned after bankruptcy. In 1917, Oscar Kraft, my great uncle, purchased Watervale as a gathering place for his extended family. It evolved into a summer resort and has been passed down through the family ever since Oscar's wifes death in 2005. Currently, Oscar's three children run the resort flawlessly.
